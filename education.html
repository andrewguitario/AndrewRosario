Here's What You Need To Get Started

learning the guitar from anywhere as never been so easy with online lessons. Forget about those pre-recorded lessons that leave you stumped, puzzled and frustrated. The best way to learn is person-to-person!

#1 Get a guitar! Fairly inexpensive guitars are available from most major music stores, and you can get something for around $150-200. I would be more than happy to help guide you with that if needed

Have your family members' or friend's old guitar? No problem! Just give it a quick dusting, and maybe slap on a set of new strings and you are ready to rock!

#2 Got a tablet and/or computer? That's all you need for online lessons. I teach preferably via two major video conference plateforms, Zoom© or Facetime©. Don't have them installed? I can help with that! It takes less than 10 minutes to install and setup this software on most android/windows, or Mac/IoS devices.
