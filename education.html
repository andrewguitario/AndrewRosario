Online Lessons: What You Need To Get Started

learning the guitar from anywhere as never been so easy with online lessons. Forget about those pre-recorded lessons that leave you stumped, puzzled and frustrated. The best way to learn is person-to-person!

#1 Get your guitar! Acoustic, nylon, electric?Doesn't matter, I specialize in variety of styles and genres, and I can work with you on just about any type of gutiar.

Don't have a guitar? Fairly inexpensive guitars are available from most major music stores, and you can get something for around $150-200. I would be more than happy to help guide you with that if needed

Have your family members' or friend's old guitar? No problem! Just give it a quick dusting, and maybe slap on a set of new strings and you are ready to rock!

#2 Got a tablet and/or computer with a webcam and mic? That's all you need for online lessons. Most tablets and laptops already come with these installed. I suggest a tablet or laptop due to the screen size, and the ability to see things a tad better than a smart phone. I teach preferably via two major video conference plateforms, Zoom© or Facetime©.

Don't have them installed? I can help with that! It takes less than 10 minutes to install and setup this software on most Android©/Windows©, or Mac©/IoS© devices.
